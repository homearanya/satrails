The Zululand Rover slackpack is a action-packed 3-day hiking adventure that
showcases the diverse coastal scenery of this captivating region. Covering coastal
mangroves, dune sand forest, wild white beaches and verdant jungles with fern-fringed
boardwalks snaking through the soaring Raphia Palm forests, this area offers an
exciting network of shady trails. \
\
These paths are perfect for leisurely birdwatching strolls or exhilarating runs. The region is renowned for its diverse birdlife, horseshoe estuary, and fine dining establishments.


Guided by bird experts and local enthusiasts passionate about the unique fauna, flora,
history and human stories that define this special place, each day on the trail promises a
new experience. 

If you choose the hike
from Amatikulu to Mtunzini, please note that it requires a moderate to high level of
fitness. Additionally, the accommodation at Amatikulu is more rustic, and the logistical
requirements make this tour package slightly more expensive.


This fully catered trail includes lodgings for 3 nights, a registered Bird guide for 2 hike
days and pathfinders/local guide for 1 hike days, various land permissions and hiking
permits, logistics, all meals, a donation towards the Mtunzini conservancy.
